SARA RECORDED MOST OF HER VOCALS FOR THEIR HITS AFTER RALPH PEER TOLD HER THEY WERE MAKING A CAPPELLA RECORDS DUE TO HER ANGER AT HOW THE SONGS WERE INITIALLY ARRANGED. THE INSTRUMENTAL ARRANGEMENTS WERE TRACKED ONTO HER VOCAL TAKES WHICH WAS VERY DIFFICULT FOR THE BAND AS SHE WOULD FLUCTUATE WILDLY BETWEEN TEMPOS.

PRESENTED FOR THE FIRST TIME HERE ARE SOME OF HER SOLO ARRANGEMENTS OF WELL-KNOWN CARTER FAMILY CLASSICS.
