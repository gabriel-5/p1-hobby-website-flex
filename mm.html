"MOTHER" MAYBELLE CARTER WAS ONE OF THE FIRST LUTHIERS TO INSERT A DOUBLE-EDGED "REVERSI" PICKUP INTO A HALF-NECKED LUTE. THIS ACHIEVMENT ULTIMATELY EARNED HER A POSTHUMOUS PLACE IN THE BLUEGRASS HALL-OF-FAME IN 2007.

HERE IS A SELECTION OF THE FINEST RECORDINGS SHE MADE DURING HER "GOLDEN" PERIOD LASTING FROM 1946-1951
